# Dread Domain Abilities - Void Playtest v1.5

## Level 1 Spells

### Blighting Strike
- **Level**: 1
- **Type**: Spell  
- **Recall Cost**: 1
- **Description**: Make a Spellcast Roll against a target within Far range. On a success, the target takes d6 magic damage using your Proficiency and the next time the target deals damage to an ally, it is reduced by 1d6. If you succeed with Fear, the target instead takes d10 magic damage using your Proficiency.

### Voice of Dread  
- **Level**: 1
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 0
- **Description**: You can magically speak directly into the ears of a creature you can see. To torment them with your words, make a Spellcast Roll against them. On a success, they must mark a Stress and become temporarily Vulnerable.

### Umbral Veil
- **Level**: 1 
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 1
- **Description**: Once per rest, when you roll with Fear, you can spend any number of Hope to place an equal number of tokens on this card, encasing yourself in a shadowy veil. After an attack roll is made against you, you can spend a number of tokens to increase it by 1d8. Additionally, when you take damage, you can spend a token to reduce it by 1d8. Each time you succeed on a token, you lose one token. On your next rest, remove all tokens from this card.

## Level 2 Spells

### Hideous Retribution
- **Level**: 2
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: When an ally within Close range takes damage from a target, you can make a Spellcast Roll against the target. On a success, mark a Stress to deal them d6 magic damage using your Proficiency.

### Siphon Essence  
- **Level**: 2
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 1
- **Description**: Make a Spellcast Roll against a target within Very Close range. On a success, once during the target takes d20 magic damage using your Proficiency. You clear a number of Hit Points equal to the number of Hit Points the target marked from this attack. On a success with Fear, you gain a +1 bonus to your Proficiency for this attack.

### Terrify
- **Level**: 2
- **Type**: Spell  
- **Recall Cost**: 1
- **Description**: Make a Spellcast Roll against a target within Far range. On a success, the target marks 1d4 Stress and you can choose to make the target run one range away from you (Very Close to Close, Close to Far, etc). On a success with Fear, the target becomes temporarily Vulnerable.

## Level 3 Spells

### Shared Trauma
- **Level**: 3
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 1
- **Description**: You can transfer suffering from one creature to another. Once per rest, mark any number of Hit Points on a willing creature within Melee range to clear an equal number of Hit Points on another willing creature within Melee range. You can choose yourself in place of either creature.

### Chains of Affliction
- **Level**: 3  
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: Mark 2 Stress to temporarily Chain a target within Close range. When a chained creature deals damage, the target of their attack reduces the number of Hit Points they mark by one. You can't have more than one creature Chained at a time.

### Summon Horror
- **Level**: 3
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: Make a Spellcast Roll against a target within Far range. On a success, spend a Hope to call forth an otherworldly creature to attack them and deal d10 magic damage using your Proficiency. The target must succeed on a Reaction Roll (12) to steel themselves from the horror or mark 1d4 Stress. After making the attack, the creature dissipates.

## Level 5 Spells

### Spectral Mist
- **Level**: 5
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 0
- **Description**: Mark a Stress to summon an eerie mist that turns you and any targets within Close range momentarily incorporeal. While a creature is incorporeal, it can move through solid objects and are immune to physical damage. A creature becomes corporeal again if it makes an attack, attempts to interact with a solid object or make an action roll. Otherwise, this effect drops at the end of the scene.

### Dire Strike
- **Level**: 5
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 1
- **Description**: After making a successful attack, you can spend 2 Hope to leech power from the target. For each Hit Point your target marked from this attack, the GM loses a Fear.

## Level 6 Spells

### Darkfire
- **Level**: 6
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: Make a Spellcast Roll against all adversaries within Close range. You can spend a Hope for any you succeed against, and they must make a Reaction Roll (14). On a failure, they take d8+6 magic damage using your Proficiency as they are engulfed in dark fire. On a success, they take half damage.

### Jump Scare
- **Level**: 6
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 0
- **Description**: When you deal magic damage to a target, you can mark a Stress to teleport from them, leaving behind them. When you do so, they become Vulnerable until they mark one or more Hit Points.

## Level 7 

### Dread-Touched
- **Level**: 7
- **Type**: Ability
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: When 4 or more of the domain cards in your loadout are from the Dread domain, gain the following benefits:
  - When you succeed with Fear, you can mark 2 Stress to prevent the GM from gaining a Fear.
  - Once per rest, when you make an action roll, you can add a +1 bonus to the roll for each Fear token the GM has scored.

### Wall of Hunger
- **Level**: 7
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: Succeed on a Spellcast Roll (13) to create a visible wall of writhing, necrotic energy at any point within Far range that lasts until you mark a Hit Point or cast this spell again. Creatures inside the wall when it appears or who try to pass through the wall must mark 2 Stress, then make a Reaction Roll (16). On a failure, they are temporarily Restrained by the wall.

## Level 8 Spells

### Dark Army
- **Level**: 8
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: Make a Spellcast Roll (14). Once per long rest, on a success you can call forth 8 tokens on this card. When you deal damage to a target in Close range, you can spend a token to increase it by 1d8. Additionally, when you take damage, you can spend a token to reduce it by 1d8. Each time you succeed on a token, you lose one token. Remove all tokens from this card on your next rest.

### Eldritch Flesh
- **Level**: 8
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 1
- **Description**: You embody the darkness you have dallied with. When this card is in your loadout:
  - Gain a +1 bonus to your damage thresholds for each Stress you have marked.
  - When you roll with Fear, you can spend 2 Hope to clear an Armor Slot.

## Level 9 Spells

### Damnation
- **Level**: 9
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: Make a Spellcast Roll against a target within Far range. On a success, mark any number of Stress to roll an equal number of d20s, dealing magic damage equal to the total rolled. If the target is defeated as a result of this attack, all adversaries within Far range of the target mark a Stress.

### Savor the Anguish
- **Level**: 9
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 0
- **Description**: When an adversary within Close range marks Stress or takes Severe damage, you can spend a Hope to clear a Stress or force the GM to lose a Fear.

## Level 10 Spells

### Invoke Torment
- **Level**: 10
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 2
- **Description**: Targets with all of their Stress marked take double damage from your attacks. Additionally, when you defeat a creature with all of its Stress marked, you clear a Stress.

### Avatar of Terror
- **Level**: 10
- **Type**: Spell
- **Recall Cost**: 1
- **Description**: Mark a Stress to transform into a creature fueled by fear. While in this form, your damage gains a +1d6 bonus for each Fear the GM has. Additionally, gain a Hope when the GM uses a Fear feature on an adversary within Close range. You must spend a Hope to make an action roll while in this form. Otherwise, you drop out of this form.
